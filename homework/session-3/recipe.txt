Title: beerbread
Ingredients: 3 cups self-rising flour, 3 tablespoons sugar, 1 can beer, 1/4 cup melted butter
Directions: Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Mix flour, sugar, and beer together. Pour into a greased loaf pan. Pour melted butter over the top. Bake for 1 hour. Let cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan.
Title: gingerbread
Ingredients: 1/2 cup white sugar, 1/2 cup butter, 1 egg, 1 cup molasses, 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda, 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 cup hot water
Directions: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 9-inch square pan. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and butter. Beat in the egg, and mix in the molasses. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves. Blend into the creamed mixture. Stir in the hot water. Pour into the prepared pan. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow to cool in pan before serving.
